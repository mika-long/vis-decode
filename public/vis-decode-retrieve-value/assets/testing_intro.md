# Task 2 --- Practice

You have completed Task 1. You will now begin **Task 2**.

On each trial, you will see a **line graph** displaying a series of data points. Your goal is to estimate the **average (mean)** value of the data shown.

To respond, use the **slider** at the bottom of the graph. As you move the slider, a **horizontal red dotted line** will appear on the graph — position it at what you think the average value is, then confirm your response by clicking the "Next" button.

Before the main trials begin, you will first complete **2 practice trials** to get familiar with the interface. These **do not count** toward your bonus.
